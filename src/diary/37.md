## The Ogulin Trip

#### 22. Mar 2026.

So, there's been one travel I haven't mentioned in last post, I went to Ogulin with my girlfrend in beginning of January. We wanted to go and see some snow for the winter. I've seen snow for two days last year on short trips, but besides that last time I really saw snow was like in elementary school and there wasnt even that much. Anyway, we stayed in a cool little colib. It was really nice and I wish I had written this sooner because It would have been a lot easyer to write all the small details where we were and what we did.

On the way back to Zadar we almost got stuck in snow because it was snowing heavily, had to take some alternate routes but I'm happy that happened because it just made the trip more fun. It was a bit uncomfortable at the time cause it was my first time driving in snow and there was lots of it. Also we didn't know if we will be able to get back as lots of roads were closing because a big snow storm was comming, but in the end... we had lots of food and time and weren't in rush to get back so we just enjoyed it.

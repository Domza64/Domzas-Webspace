## Adv riding is epic!

#### 23. Jul 2025.

Recently I went to a few ultra short trips on my bike. Probably can't even call them that; they were all just a day of riding and were mostly just around Zadar but still... it was amazing just going arround; exploring random dirt and gravel paths. This kind of dual-sport riding is definitely what I wanna do in the future.

Now I did kind of buy a lot of other things recently so I won't be selling my Yamaha just yet. But I still plan to replace it with a CRF 300L at some point in the near future.

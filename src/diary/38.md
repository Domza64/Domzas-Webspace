## GTA V

#### 18. Apr 2026.

So recently I've been re-playing GTA V and it's fucking fun! Now I don't really play games these days and when I used to play every day, I didn't play lots of different ones, so I'm a pretty bad judge. Hearing about all the new games and technologies, I didn't expect much of going back to GTA V on a PS3, but to me, it's still just super impressive. I mean, except for a few frame drops and studders here and there, it's perfectly playable and beautiful. The more I played it, my expectations for GTA 6 were just dropping lower and lower to the point where I'm just not that excited anymore.

I mean I definitely wanna fucking try out GTA 6 when it comes out and I will. Not too soon, as I don't have or plan to get a PS5, so I'll wait for a PC release.
When it comes to gameplay, graphics, map size, details and literally everything technical, I have absolutely no doubt that it will be anything short of amazing and like the best game ever...

But as I said. At this point, I actually expect very little. Replaying GTA V (as a young adult, not a kid) has made me realize how "edgy", "unsafe", "offensive" it actually is. Of course, I'm talking about singleplayer, not these shit new online updates. And this all applies to older GTA games. I played San Andreas and IV, but I'm pretty sure that it's pretty similar to all older GTA games. Anyway, I just can't see anything even remotely similar to any of that being released in today's world. All mainstream games are just colorful and safe garbage made to appeal to kids, or have these overly sexual characters to appeal to teenagers. Now to think that GTA VI is going to have a good story with these edgy, strong and offensive characters is just insane. And really, that is what made those games fun, not just graphics.

I really fucking hope I'm wrong. I want GTA VI to be the best GTA ever, but I don't think it can. At least not today, when everything is made to be "safe" just to have bigger adoption potential that ultimately just makes more money.

GTA VI will probably be fun and insanely impressive to just drive around, but that's about it.

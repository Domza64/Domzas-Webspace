How I started?

Early days:

I became interested in programming when I was around 13-14 years old. Back then I was really stupid, I was just a kid that wanted to make his own video games. I was also interested in electronics so I was kind of playing around with programming arduino boards (just basic blinking leds and basic stuff...).

How I actually got started:

Since I was also interested in photography and video making, I unfortunately never really got into programming... Not until late 2021 when I was around 18 years old.
Because of other hobbyes and obligations I wasn't really making a lot of progress, but I was slowly learing and enjoying every second of it (except few million bugs and keyboard braking moments).
